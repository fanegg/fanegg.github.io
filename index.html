Yue Chen (陈悦)

I am a first-year PhD student at Westlake University and Zhejiang University, co-supervised by Yuliang Xiu and Gerard Pons-Moll.

I am interested in modeling 3D/4D worlds from casually captured real-world images and videos. Currently, I am working closely with Anpei Chen at Inception3D Lab, and have previously interned at Tencent AI Lab, collaborating with Xuan Wang. I completed my M.S. at Xi'an Jiaotong University and my B.S. at Xiamen University.

As a Muggle dreaming of the Wizarding World, I feel so fortunate to create magic through what I am exploring on this jorney.
